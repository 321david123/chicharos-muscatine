Nuestra Historia

A little family kitchen with a whole lot of flavor.

Chicharo's is a family-owned Mexican grill in the heart of Muscatine — the kind of place where the salsas are fire-roasted in house, the corn chips are cut and fried fresh, and the masa for our tamales is made by hand. No shortcuts, just the recipes our familia grew up on.

Regulars come back for the tacos de asada, the loaded burritos, and a queso worth the drive. It's honest, generous, made-to-order Mexican food — and it's why folks around town keep calling us the best tacos they've had.
